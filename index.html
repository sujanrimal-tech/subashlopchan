Featured Projects

Kiln Drive VFD System Retrofit

Location: Hetauda Cement Industries

Challenge: The main kiln drive was powered by an inefficient, high-maintenance slip-ring motor system, causing frequent downtime and high energy costs.

Solution: I led a project to replace the outdated system with a modern 2.5 MW Siemens GH180 VFD and a high-efficiency induction motor. This involved complete system design, power & control cable scheduling, PLC integration, and commissioning.

Key Outcomes:

  • Achieved a 22% reduction in energy consumption for the kiln drive.
  • Provided precise speed control, improving product quality.
  • Drastically reduced maintenance hours and costs.

Automated Warehouse Lighting System

Client: CG Foods (Wai Wai) at CG Industrial Park

Challenge: A 10,000 sq. meter warehouse had inefficient high-bay lighting that was left on 24/7, leading to massive energy waste.

Solution: I designed and commissioned a fully automated DALI-based LED lighting system. The system used occupancy sensors and daylight harvesting to ensure lights were only on when and where needed, and at the appropriate brightness level.

Key Outcomes:

  • Cut warehouse lighting costs by over 60%.
  • Improved safety with better-lit aisles during operation.
  • Provided a scalable solution that was adopted in other warehouses.
